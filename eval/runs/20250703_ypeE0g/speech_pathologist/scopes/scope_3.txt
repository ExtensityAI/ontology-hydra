1. Domain Definition
1.1 Speech Pathology
- Speech pathology encompasses the evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment of communication and swallowing disorders.
- The domain addresses conditions affecting speech fluency, voice, language, and cognitive communication disorders, as well as swallowing disorders.

3. Terminology
3.1 Domain-Specific Terms
- Stuttering: A speech disorder characterized by repeated or prolonged sounds, syllables, or words.
- Stuttering Severity Instrument: A tool used to assess the severity of stuttering.
- CAPE-V: Consensus Auditory-Perceptual Evaluation of Voice, used for voice disorder assessment.
- Voice Handicap Index: A questionnaire assessing the emotional and functional impact of voice disorders.
